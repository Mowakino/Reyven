PuddingFest, an annual occasion orchestrated by PT ChipiChapa, exhibits the most recent advancements from a plethora of esteemed companies across Indonesia. This virtual event comprises multiple segments allocated for each participating entity to unveil their groundbreaking innovations. Those intrigued by these forefront technologies must enroll via the PuddingFest portal. Spearheaded by CEO Pak Raja, PT ChipiChapa endeavors to furnish a stage for industry pioneers to unveil their progressions and stimulate cooperation within the technological sphere.
